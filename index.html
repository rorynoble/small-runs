Process

How It Works

We keep it simple. No minimums that make small artists feel small. Just great merch, fast.

01

Get a Quote

Tell us what you need — garment type, quantity, size breakdown, and your artwork. We'll come back to you fast with pricing.

02

Approve Your Proof

We'll send a mockup so you can sign off on placement, colours, and sizing before we press a single item.

03

We Press

Once approved, we get to work. Heat press production typically takes 1–2 days depending on order size.

04

Pick Up or Ship

Collect in Auckland or Melbourne, or we'll ship anywhere across New Zealand and Australia. 4–5 days total, start to finish.
